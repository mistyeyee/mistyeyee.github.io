Matcha is a high-grade green tea ground into powdered form.

The green tea powder is whisked into hot water, instead of steeped, to form a frothy drink.

It is finely ground powder of specially grown and processed green tea leaves originating in China

traditionally consumed in East Asia. It is mostly produced in Japan today.
